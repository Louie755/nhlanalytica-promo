The Impact
Rating Explained

Most hockey stats tell you what happened. Impact Rating (IR) tells you who made it happen. It's a weighted composite that accounts for production, ice time, situational play, and team context — compressing everything into one number you can actually use.

Built and maintained by Louie Suh, a 12-year competitive hockey player and NYU Sports Management student who grew up watching hockey as an outsider — and decided to build the tool he always wanted.
